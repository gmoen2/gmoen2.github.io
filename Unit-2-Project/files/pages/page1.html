The Unity Engine

Pros

Unity is more simplistic than most other game engines. Many people use it as their first stepping stones. Indie developers see it as a perfect fit for them. Unity has seen the most successful indie games from it's engine, and it's free.

Cons

Unity great cons are some of license aggreements, they arn't very direct and you should read the fine print before signing your life away. They may not give you as much freedom when it comes to selling your game as other game engines. As well as that, their graphics are still a little behind other, although it doesn't take great graphics to make a great game.
